Introduction

Hey there Jazz lovers this is Herbie Adderley. I am jazz live recordings collecter and is interested in sharing my vast collection of records to the interested people.This is my own business and I started it in 1999. If there is any query people please do not hesitate to contact me.

About Us

Mr. Herbie Adderley who is a resident of 64 Manchester Street, Christchurch started this project The Jazz Live.. Herbie is a jazz enthusiastic who loves collecting the Jazz Music. His love for jazz music made him set up a small business which used to collect the live jazz recording in 1999. Now, after years of collecting live jazz recording, he feels it is time for him to share his knowledge and information about jazz music to other jazz enthusiastic like him. Hence, here is the project The Jazz Live.

Our Goal

The main goal of this website is to share the live jazz recordings done by varioius artists and their work. This is a project for all the music lovers especially jazz music lover to listen to and get the information about the jazz music. The user can go through various artists and recordings and watch videos as well.
